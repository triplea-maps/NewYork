== GangLand New York City ==

Made by Anthony Rafanello

Build your turf and crush the competition. Based loosely on the Warriors movie.

Only one leader unit per game

Lose your money when you lose last capital, PU's destroyed, not captured. Can still produce after one turn without capital.

Each side has these techs for gameplay: improvedArtillerySupport,paratroopers,mechanizedInfantry, rockets. Added victory conditions of controlling 8 capitals-Roger Cooper
